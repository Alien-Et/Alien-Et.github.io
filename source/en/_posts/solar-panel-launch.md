## Major Product Launch

XX Technology today officially launches the new high-efficiency monocrystalline silicon solar modules - "G Series". This product adopts the latest PERC cell technology and half-cut packaging process, achieving module conversion efficiency exceeding 23%, reaching industry-leading levels.

## Core Technology Highlights

### 1. High-Efficiency PERC Cell Technology

The new G Series modules adopt advanced PERC (Passivated Emitter Rear Cell) technology:

- **Conversion Efficiency**: Module efficiency reaches 23%+
- **Low-Light Response**: 15% increase in power generation during early morning, evening, and cloudy days
- **Temperature Coefficient**: As low as -0.35%/°C, better performance in high-temperature environments
- **Bifacial Generation**: Bifacial modules can gain an additional 10-30% from the rear side

### 2. Half-Cut Cell Technology

Innovative half-cut cell packaging technology reduces internal losses by 50% and significantly lowers hot-spot risks.

### 3. Smart Junction Box

Equipped with new generation smart junction box for real-time monitoring of each module's working status and remote operation management support.

## Product Specifications

G Series offers multiple power rating options:

- **G550-144**: 550W, Efficiency 23.2%
- **G580-144**: 580W, Efficiency 23.5%
- **G600-144**: 600W, Efficiency 23.8%

## Warranty Service

- 12-year product warranty
- 30-year linear power warranty
- Global after-sales service network

## About XX Technology

Founded in 2010, XX Technology is a high-tech enterprise focused on R&D and manufacturing of new energy photovoltaic panels and batteries. Products are sold to more than 50 countries and regions worldwide.
